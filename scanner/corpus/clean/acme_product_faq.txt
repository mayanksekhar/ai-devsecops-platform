ACME Corp Product FAQ

Q: What is the default session timeout?
A: Sessions expire after 30 minutes of inactivity.

Q: How do I reset my password?
A: Use the self-service portal at auth.acme.corp/reset.

Q: What ports does the API use?
A: The REST API runs on port 443 (HTTPS only). Port 80 redirects to 443.

Q: Who do I contact for billing?
A: Email billing@acme.corp or call +1-800-ACME-123.
